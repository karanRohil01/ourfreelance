Frequently Asked Questions

What is your development process?

We follow an agile development methodology, working in two-week sprints. This allows for flexibility, regular feedback through demos, and ensures the final product is perfectly aligned with your vision.

How do I get started with a project?

The first step is a free, no-obligation discovery call. We'll discuss your idea, goals, and challenges. From there, we'll prepare a detailed project proposal and roadmap for your review.

Do you offer support after the project is launched?

Yes! We offer a range of ongoing support and maintenance packages to ensure your application remains secure, updated, and performs optimally. We believe in long-term partnerships.

Can you take over an existing project?

Absolutely. We have extensive experience in auditing, refactoring, and modernizing existing codebases. We can help you rescue a failing project or add new features to a mature application.

Do I own the source code?

Yes. Upon final payment and project completion, you retain 100% ownership of the intellectual property and the source code for your project.
